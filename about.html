About Bettorr

Bettorr is a modern, minimalistic web interface for searching and downloading PC game torrents from multiple trusted sources. Our mission is to make game discovery and downloading fast, beautiful, and accessible for everyone—without ads, tracking, or registration.

Project Mission

  • Provide a clean, user-friendly torrent search experience
  • Aggregate results from trusted repack sources
  • Respect user privacy—no tracking, no accounts
  • Open source and community-driven
